About DEWY

Your Intelligent Skin Diagnostic Assistant

DEWY is an advanced skin condition diagnostic platform that combines cutting-edge AI technology with dermatological expertise to provide accurate and accessible skin health assessments.

How It Works

Image Analysis

Our image-based diagnostic system uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on thousands of dermatological images. The model can identify common skin conditions including:

  • Acne
  • Eksim (Eczema)
  • Herpes
  • Panu (Tinea versicolor)
  • Rosacea

The model has been trained to an accuracy of over 90% on validated test cases.

Questionnaire Assessment

Our text-based diagnostic system uses a sophisticated machine learning algorithm trained on clinical data. The questionnaire evaluates factors such as:

  • Demographics (age, skin type)
  • Symptoms and duration
  • Medical history
  • Lifestyle factors
  • Environmental exposure

This provides a complementary diagnostic approach for users who prefer not to upload photos or want a second opinion.

Our Technology

Image Model

Our CNN-based image classification model was built using TensorFlow and Keras. The model architecture includes:

  • Multiple convolutional layers with ReLU activation
  • Max pooling layers for feature selection
  • Dropout layers to prevent overfitting
  • Batch normalization for training stability
  • Dense layers for final classification

Text-based Model

Our questionnaire-based model was built using scikit-learn. The model includes:

  • Feature preprocessing and normalization
  • Feature selection to identify key predictors
  • Random Forest classifier for robust prediction
  • Cross-validation to ensure generalizability
  • Probability calibration for confidence scoring

Medical Disclaimer

DEWY is designed to be an informational and preliminary diagnostic tool only. It is not intended to replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

While our AI models have been trained on extensive datasets, they have limitations and may not accurately identify all skin conditions. The recommendations provided are general in nature and may not be suitable for everyone.

If you experience severe symptoms, rapid worsening of a condition, or any concerning changes to your skin, please consult a healthcare professional immediately.
